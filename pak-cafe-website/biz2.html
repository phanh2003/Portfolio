PAK Cafe & Bakery

PAK Cafe & Bakery is a small personal coffee shop, newly established in 2023. The cafe includes drinks suitable for children, adults, and elders. The shop also has cakes and pastries so that customers can eat cakes and enjoy special drinks from PAK Cafe and Bakery. PAK is ideal for those who want to try new and unique coffee flavors. PAK Cafe is inspired by ingredients that seem impossible to combine with coffee, but they will make you say Wow when you try them.

BAKERY KEY FEATURES

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Available for dietary
  • Non-dairy Products

Banh Tieu La Dua; Vietnamese Pandan Hollow Donut
Vietnamese Pandan Hollow Donut is a donut made from whole wheat flour with pandan leaves liquid, water, and yeast, fried until turn into golden brown in an air fryer. Vietnamese Pandan Hollow Donut is not too sweet like traditional Western donuts and is covered with a layer of white sesame. Because of its low sweetness, the Vietnamese Pandan Hollow Donut can go well with all kinds of drinks at PAK.
$2.5 each donut

Banh Bo Nuong; Palm Sugar Honeycomb Cake
Banh Bo is a type of rice cake made from: rice flour, water, palm sugar, coconut milk, and yeast. The cake's surface has many small bubbles due to the many air holes in the cake. Banh Bo is one of the most famous Vietnamese desserts. Palm Sugar Honeycomb Cake has a sweet taste from palm sugar and coconut milk, which can be suitable for all ages. Because Banh Bo is sweeter than Banh Tieu, it is often used with salty coffee to neutralize the taste.
$2.5 each piece

Banh Mi Cham Sua; Vietnamese Kid's breakfast
Banh Mi Cham Sua is simply small baguettes dipped in condensed milk. This dish is the childhood of many children in Vietnam. Condensed milk tastes sweet when eaten with bread, creating a slight sweetness in the mouth. Although the bread is not big, it will fill you up for a working day. This is a great choice for you if you want to try a signature Vietnamese breakfast.
$3 each
