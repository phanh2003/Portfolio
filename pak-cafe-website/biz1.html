PAK Cafe & Bakery

PAK Cafe & Bakery is a small personal coffee shop, newly established in 2023. The cafe includes drinks suitable for children, adults, and elders. The shop also has cakes and bread so that customers can eat cakes and enjoy special drinks from PAK Cafe and Bakery. PAK is ideal for those who want to try new and unique coffee flavors. PAK Cafe is inspired by ingredients that seem impossible to combine with coffee, but they will make you say Wow when you try them.

KEY FEATURES

  • Real Natural Ingredients
  • Pure Coffee Imported from Vietnam
  • Non-dairy Products
  • Non-caffeinated Drink

Ca Phe Cot Dua; Coconut Coffee
The combination of coconut milk slush and pure Vietnamese expresso. The sweetness is made from coconut sugar. We can taste the creamy from coconut milk, the bitterness from the expresso, and the sweetness from coconut sugar.
Never go wrong for Coconut lovers.
$8.5

Com Xanh Cot Dua; Vietnamese Green Rice Coconut Milk Slush
The combination of coconut milk slush and green rice nuggets. Green nuggets is a dish made from young glutinous rice roasted with the shell and pounded many times; the finished product is wrapped in 2 layers of leaves, so it is green. Com is a bit sweet and has a fleshy taste. After buying, the nuggets will be stir-fried with a little coconut sugar, then blended with coconut milk and ice to create a drink. This is one of the unique drinks at PAK Cafe and Bakery. It's for people who want to try unfamiliar drinks and those who don't want caffeinated drinks.
Available for everyone, have only at PAK.
$9.5

Ca Phe Muoi; Salty Coffee
Salty coffee is a drink made from coffee, condensed milk, whipped cream, and table salt. The salty taste of salt does not seem to fit in a cup of coffee, but it highlights the passionate flavor, softens the bitterness of coffee, and increases the sweetness of the milk. The salty taste of salt can inhibit the bitter and sour taste of coffee so that you will feel less bitter and create a more delicious feeling when drinking. The salt is mixed with the whipped cream liquid until it becomes a salty foam that is added to the top of the coffee.
The best unique coffee. Never go wrong for Coffee lovers.
$9.5
